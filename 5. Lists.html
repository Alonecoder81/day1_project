Angela's Recipe for the Best Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 3/4 cup warm milk
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons yeast
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

For the filling

  • 2/3 cup dark brown suger
  • 1 1/2 tablespoon groound chinnamon
  • 1/4 cup butter

Instructions

  1. MIx the milk with the yeast suger,aggs.
  2. Melt the butter and add to the mixture
  3. add in the flour and mix until combined into a dough.
  4. Knead the dough for 10 minute.
  5. Transfer the dough into a large bowl and cover with plastic wrap.leave it somewhere to rise for 2 hours.
